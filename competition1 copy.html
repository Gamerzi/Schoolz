Inter school cricket competition

Teams: 12 teams, carefully selected based on their performance in local leagues, face off in a knockout-style format.

Venues: The matches take place at DAV public school, where roaring crowds create an electric atmosphere.

Intensity: From the opening whistle to the final penalty shootout, every moment is charged with adrenaline. Tackles, dribbles, and breathtaking goals keep fans on the edge of their seats.

Underdog Stories: Watch out for those underdog teams—the ones with heart and hunger—who defy expectations and challenge the favorites.

Golden Bat Race: Strikers battle it out for the coveted Golden Boot award, celebrating the top goal scorer of the tournament.

Passion Beyond the Pitch: Fans paint their faces, wave banners, and chant anthems. Rivalries ignite, friendships form, and memories are etched forever.
